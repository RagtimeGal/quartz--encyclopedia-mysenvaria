The Relatives Star is the host [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Geography/Stars|star]] of the [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Geography/Stars/Star Systems/Relatives Star System|Relatives Star System]]. The star, created by the [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/History/Biographies/Gods/Phenomena Gods/Gods of Weather/God of Heat Lightning|God of Heat Lightning]], documents relations held by themself and other related [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Biology/Sophonts/Gods|Gods]] between a number of [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Biology/Sophonts/Human|humans]], [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Biology/Sophonts/Dwarf|dwarves]], and even [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Biology/Sophonts/Elf|elves]].
