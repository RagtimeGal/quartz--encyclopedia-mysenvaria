Hydrogen Cyanide's Plants is the host [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Geography/Phenomena/Stars|star]] of the [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Geography/Stars/Star Systems/Plant Star System|Plant Star System]]. Following the events of the [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/History/God-War Era/God Councils|First God Council]] the [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/History/Biographies/Gods/Compound Gods/God of Hydrogen Cyanide|God of Hydrogen Cyanide]] would create the star to document their creation of the first [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Biology/Taxa/Plant|plant]], and algae-like asexual [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Science/Principles/Organisms|organism]].
