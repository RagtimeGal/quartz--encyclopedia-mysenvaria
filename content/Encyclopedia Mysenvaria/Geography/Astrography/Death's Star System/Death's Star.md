Death's Star is the host [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Culture/Sciences & Technologies/Scientific Phenomena/Stars|star]] of [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/Geography/Astrography/Star Systems/Death's Star System|Death's Star System]]. Created by an unknown God the star documents their fight with and the ensuing death of another unknown God. The star marks the beginning of the [[Encyclopedia Mysenvaria/History/God-War Era/God-War Era|God-War]].
